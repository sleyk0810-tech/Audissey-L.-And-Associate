Corporate Law

New governance standards, cross-border diligence, and shareholder oversight

12 June 2026 · 6 min read

The business landscape continues to evolve rapidly, with corporate law hot spots emerging in sustainability reporting, digital asset compliance, and multi-jurisdictional transaction oversight. Boards are under pressure to modernize governance frameworks while staying aligned with investor expectations and regulatory change.

For Irish and international counsel, priority areas include designing practical disclosure controls, reviewing cross-border share purchase agreements, and advising on board-level accountability for ESG and anti-corruption programs.
